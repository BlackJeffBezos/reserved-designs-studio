Overview

Never lose a good idea again

02 / 10 — THE PROBLEM

The Problem

Ideas are lost in friction

Even the best digital tools introduce costly delays at the moment of inspiration. Unlocking a phone, opening Notion or Obsidian, navigating folders, and hitting record breaks creative flow — and causes ideas to vanish before they are ever captured.

The friction chain Unlock phone → Find app → Open app → Navigate to new note → Tap record → Speak. By step three, the idea is already degrading. The tool designed to save ideas is the reason they're lost.

03 / 10 — THE SOLUTION

The Solution

One press. Zero friction.

Queen reduces idea capture to its absolute essence. No screen. No notifications. No distractions. Just the most direct path from thought to preservation ever designed into a desk object.

1
Think An idea arrives — at 1:17 AM, in the shower, mid-meeting, or mid-run. The moment is fleeting.
2
Press the Queen's crown One tactile press on the crown — no unlock, no navigation, no app. Recording begins instantly.
3
Speak The INMP441 MEMS microphone captures your voice with clarity. The idea is recorded locally to microSD — immediately and privately.
4
Done — auto-upload on trusted Wi-Fi When connected to your trusted Wi-Fi network, Queen securely auto-uploads recordings to your private encrypted cloud storage. Your ideas, your server, your control.

08 / 10 — PRIVACY & CONTROL

Privacy

Your ideas, your storage, your rules

Queen rejects the always-listening surveillance model. In a world of cloud-dependent tools and AI processors that treat user data as a product, Queen takes the opposite position: your ideas never leave your control unless you choose otherwise.

Privacy-first architecture Recordings remain on the local microSD card at all times. Upload only triggers when connected to a Wi-Fi network the user has explicitly marked as trusted. The destination is the user's own private encrypted cloud storage — not a shared service, not a third-party AI processor.
